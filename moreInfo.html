Assistance and Support Center

08:00AM to 10:00PM

055 112 7777

Our dedicated park staff is trained to provide assistance and support to visitors with disabilities. From providing directions and information to offering guidance and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience, our team is here to help.

  • Sensory Considerations

    We understand that some visitors may have sensory sensitivities. To create a more inclusive environment, we strive to minimize excessive noise levels and provide quiet spaces for individuals who may need a break from the sensory stimulation.

  • Service Animals

    Certified service animals are welcome at Minecraft Themepark. We kindly request that service animals remain on a leash or harness at all times and that their owners adhere to local regulations and guidelines.

Accessible Parking and Restrooms

  • Accessible parking spaces are available near the entrance of the park, providing convenient access for visitors with mobility challenges. These designated spaces are located in close proximity to the park entrance, allowing for easier navigation.

  • Accessible restroom facilities equipped with spacious stalls and grab bars are conveniently located throughout the park. These facilities are designed to accommodate the needs of individuals with disabilities, ensuring comfort and convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any additional questions or specific inquiries regarding getting to Minecraft Themepark, please don't hesitate to contact our Guest Services team. We are here to assist you and ensure a smooth and enjoyable visit to our park.

How early should I arrive at the park? We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the park opens to allow for parking, ticketing, and entry procedures. This will ensure you have ample time to fully enjoy your day at Minecraft Themepark.
Are there any age restrictions for entering the park? The park welcomes visitors of all ages. However, children under 8 must be accompanied by an adult.
Can I bring my own food and drinks into the park? Outside food and drinks are not permitted within the park premises. However, we offer a wide range of dining options and food outlets that cater to various tastes and dietary preferences.
Can I re-enter the park if I need to leave temporarily? Yes, you are permitted to re-enter the park on the same day, provided you have your valid ticket or wristband and have it scanned upon re-entry.
Are there storage facilities available for personal belongings? Yes, we provide secure storage lockers at convenient locations within the park. You can rent these lockers to store your personal belongings and enjoy the park without the need to carry them around.
Are there any age restrictions for entry to Minecraft Themepark? Minecraft Themepark welcomes visitors of all ages. There are no specific age restrictions for entry, and we have attractions and experiences suitable for guests of different age groups.
